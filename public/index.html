Objective

One side plans the perfect crime. The other hunts them down.

🦹 Robbers

Each robber has a secret Mission Card — reach the target, complete the heist, then escape through a designated exit before getting caught.

👮 Police

Officers work together to corner and arrest all robbers. Draw Police Cards each turn for special powers.

🏙️ The City

A procedurally generated 20×16 grid of roads, alleys, buildings and locations. Every seed is a new city.

⏱️ Round Limit

Game ends after the round limit. Any escape = robbers win. All arrested = police win.

Movement

Roll a d6 each turn — spend every move or lose them.

🎲 Rolling

Click Roll Dice. You must use all movement — plan your path carefully before committing.

↕️ Alleys

Dark passages cost 2 movement points. They're shortcuts across districts but drain your roll fast. Crowbar and Skeleton Key reduce this.

🚧 Roadblocks

Police can place hard blocks on road squares. Cannot be passed — use alternate routes or Smoke Bomb.

◈ Tool Tiles

Landing on a diamond tile draws a Tool Card. These are the robber's main advantage — don't skip them.

Heists

The job isn't done until the loot is in hand and you're out the door.

📍 Target

Your Mission Card names a specific location. Move to that square and the Heist button activates.

🎲 Heist Roll

Roll a d6 — meet or beat the difficulty threshold. Success grabs the loot. Failure increases heat.

💼 Carrying Loot

Once you have loot, movement is reduced by 1. Get to your exit before police close in.

🚪 Exits

Your mission card designates valid exit squares on the board edge. Step on one with loot and you escape.

Heat

The more you're noticed, the harder it gets.

🌡️ What Is Heat?

Heat is your wanted level. It rises with failed heists, close encounters, and warrants. It never decreases.

⚠️ Heat 2+

Dice roll capped at 4. You can't sprint when half the precinct knows your face.

🚨 Heat 3+

An extra officer spawns at the nearest station. More heat = more boots on the ground.

🎭 Disguise

Lets you pass through an officer without arrest. Burns on direct confrontation.

Cards

Tool Cards are the robber's edge. Police Cards are the law's long arm.
Pre-Roll play before rolling  ·  Post-Roll after rolling, before moving  ·  Post-Move after movement ends
Tool Cards — Robbers Only
Drawn when landing on a ◈ tile. Hold up to 3.
Police Cards — Officers Only
Drawn at start of each police turn. Applied immediately.

Winning

Only one side walks away clean.

🦹 Robbers Win

At least one robber reaches a valid exit with loot. One escape is enough — others being caught doesn't matter.

👮 Police Win

All robbers are arrested before the round limit. Every robber must be in custody.

⏱️ Time Limit

If time expires with any free robber, it's a draw — robbers escape on a technicality.

🎲 Slip-Away

When cornered, roll a 6 to escape. 5-in-6 odds of arrest — don't let it come to this.
